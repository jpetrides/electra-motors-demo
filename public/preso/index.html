Under the Hood

How identity resolution works

1
Web SDK assigns a device ID to every visitor
A unique anonymous identifier per browser — no login required
2
Behavioral events stream in real time
Page views, product views, vehicle configurations — all captured as they happen
3
Lead submission fires identity signals
Email, phone, and name are sent to Data Cloud alongside the vehicle interest
4
Data Cloud reconciliation rules match and merge
Anonymous device ID is linked to the matching CRM contact — creating one Unified Individual
5
Unified profile surfaces full CRM enrichment
Address, dealer history, MDM ID, and every vehicle lead — all in one view, available to every Salesforce app
