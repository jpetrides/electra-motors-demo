About the Regulator

Pure electric. No compromises.

The Regulator wasn't adapted from an ICE platform. It was designed from the ground up as an electric vehicle — which is why the numbers look impossible until you're behind the wheel. 400 miles of range, 800-volt architecture for ultra-fast charging, and a tri-motor AWD system that puts power exactly where it needs to be.

Software-defined: the Regulator Performance receives over-the-air updates that improve performance, range, and features over time. Your car on delivery day is not your car a year later — it's better.
