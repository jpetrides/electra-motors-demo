About the Reaktive

Built for the journey ahead.

The Reaktive Touring seats seven without sacrificing performance. Engineered with a dual-motor AWD system capable of 402 horsepower, the Reaktive handles mountain roads and daily school runs with equal confidence.

The panoramic glass roof stretches the full length of the cabin. The adaptive air suspension reads road conditions 500 times per second. And the 12.3-inch driver display keeps every metric within a glance — not a reach.
